﻿A lot of people claim to create designs that resonate with the user mentality but I believe every user looks at a design with a unique perspective and there’s no set pattern.

As a designer with 3+ years of experience, I’ve observed users of different ages and worked with unique sets of brands. My solution is more of a holistic approach and that is to provide the user exactly what they’re here for, nothing less or nothing more.

People have an attention span of less than 5 sec while looking at a design and my goal is to make them explore so that they keep coming for more.
